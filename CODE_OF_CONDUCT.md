# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, or other similar characteristics.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in your communication
- Assume good intentions from other community members
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing conduct@yenchat.com. All reports will be handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Community leaders will take appropriate action in response to any behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful. This may include warning the offender, temporary ban, or permanent ban from the community.
